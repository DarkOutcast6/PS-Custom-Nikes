About Us

Nikes is one of - if not the biggest - shoe brands in the world. They have hundreds of shoes for a variety of ages and feet, but they lack one vital resource; custom kid shoes. That's where we come in.We fill the gap of custom kid Nikes. With classy and creative shoe designs, you'll find everything from eye-catching designs to exclusive and elegant shoe designs.

Though we are a company, we have one unsual thing about us; we are all kids. From coding this websites to spending hours decorating shoes for your pleasure, we do it all. We hope you enjoy our services and shoes. If anything is not meeting you needs, feel free to email us!
